this blog

I’m not sure what to write here? It’s just another blog. I write about my opinions and likes and dislikes and put them here. Expect mostly CS-related stuff here, mixed in with some of my occasional stupid random thoughts that must be shared with the internet*.

me

My name’s M------ S----, but I go by quantomistro in most places on the internet. I don’t remember why I chose “quantomistro”, but I’m pretty sure it involved some random word generators and my interest in astronomy and quantum-anything. I was born in January of 2003**. I love learning new things, and get easily engrossed in anything I find cool. I hate the feeling of not knowing or understanding or being ignorant about something. My favorite colors are #9dffbe and #ff3f69; I prefer putting cereal in my bowl before milk; my favorite subjects are math, physics, and computer science; I don’t have any subjects I particularly dislike; I love open-source software; I like programming in Rust and Elixir and TypeScript; my favorite author (at least right now) is Dan Brown; I love watching football/soccer and cricket; my favorite football team is Manchester United; I love learning about anything quantum-related (did I already mention that?); I can’t think of anything else right now, so I’ll add more to this later.
